Life

Hi there :)

I grew up in Saigon, the heart of southern Vietnam. In 2009, I left for higher education in Singapore, living my 20s in the city island till this day. In 2013 I graduated from National University of Singapore (NUS), with BSc (Hons) in Computer Science.

Profession wise, I am a Zend Certified PHP Engineer, full-stack developer and winner of Edtech Asia Hackathon 2016.

In personal life, I have a loving family, from whom I witnessed love and sacrifice. I am thankful for the all great people and friendships I have in life.

Passion

I have been through many types of work and I found my passion for both education and programming. I am interested in STEM learning for young learners. I want to enable kids to think and expand their creativity through programming.

There are many underprivileged kids in Vietnam lack of resources and opportunities to build career and contribute to society. And they end up wasting their life on drugs or cheap overseas labouring.

I used to teach various programming courses as teaching assistants while I was in university. For years I have been conducting voluntary training courses for the Vietnamese labour workers in Johor Bahru, Malaysia. I love combining IT and education, love seeing people grow.

On the side, I make music, backpack travel, hack and blog about various stuff.
